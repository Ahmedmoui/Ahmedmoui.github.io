# Autonomous RC Aircraft – Design Build Fly Capstone

## Problem
The OSU Design Build Fly (DBF) team needed to achieve autonomous flight capability for their competition aircraft — a first for the team. The challenge was integrating a reliable autopilot system into a custom-built RC aircraft and validating it through actual flight testing.

## What I Did

### Autonomous Systems Lead
Led the autonomous sub-team responsible for selecting, configuring, and validating the autopilot stack:
- Configured an **ArduPilot-based autopilot** system for the aircraft's flight dynamics
- Used **Mission Planner** for avionics configuration, pre-flight checks, and live telemetry monitoring
- Defined and tested autonomous mission waypoints

### Hardware Build
Hands-on fabrication of the aircraft from the ground up:
- Frame assembly and structural build
- Full avionics and electrical wiring
- Extensive soldering of flight controller, ESCs, receivers, and power distribution components

## Results
Achieved the team's **first successful autonomous test flight** — validating the autopilot integration and autonomous mission execution.
