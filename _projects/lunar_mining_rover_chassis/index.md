# Lunar Mining Rover Chassis

## Problem
The LBCC Space Exploration Club was designing a lunar mining rover concept and needed a chassis and motor mount system that could survive rough terrain, accommodate drive motors, and be iterated on quickly with limited resources.

## What I Did

### CAD Design
Designed the full rover frame and motor mount assemblies in CAD — focusing on structural integrity, motor alignment, and manufacturability.

### Prototype Iteration
Used **3D printed prototypes** to validate fit and function at each design stage. Physical testing of each iteration informed dimensional adjustments and joint reinforcements before moving to the next version.

## Outcome
Produced a chassis design that met the team's structural and spatial requirements, with a documented iteration history from initial concept to final prototype.
